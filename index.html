know me better

Hi, I am Bartosz, Product Designer from Poland. The job title doesn't tell you anything? I know, just call me Designer. Just like I tell my family what I do for a living, I'll tell you - I design interfaces for websites, web and mobile applications.

In addition to taking care of the visual appearance, I do a lot of process and research that goes into the design concept. Each product must be carefully thought out so that it is usable, fully accessible to everyone, and at the same time looks modern so that it is ready for use.
