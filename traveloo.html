Why I made this products?

As a big fan of travel, I've created a product that offers a wide range of homes for rent while traveling. We have many beautiful places in the world, so let's also make sure we live in one. Many people are also looking for their own places on earth, Traveloo can help them with that.

Design Assumptions

Case Study/Texts/Bold Lead

Increasing number of people wanting peace and quiet away from crowded places

The goal is to increase liquidity in the rental real estate market, making it easy for users to book

I intend to build a product that assists the user in searching for a house for rent

Unique value

People are missing a place where they can feel "at home," so the product will target a group of people looking for houses to rent in beautiful places around the world

Source: GCDL

Competitive analysis

I analyzed 3 brands offering similar products. I focused on current trends that competitors have applied and user needs.

Airbnb

The good

Setting modern trends. Brilliantly constructed - Airbnb.design

Large community and trust built

Offering various types of accommodation

The bad

Reviews of cancellations or non-refunds.

Agoda

The good

Wide range of hotels available

Many destinations where we can book our accommodation

The bad

There are few options to rent secluded and private places.

Booking.com

The good

Huge confidence in the market

Most hotels and places are available at

The bad

Compared to others, the brand does not tap into modern trends

Typography

As it is aimed at a new market, Sans typeface will do a great job as one of the most eye-friendly typeface.

For the design, I used different font weights to add variety to the product and hierarchy to the elements.

The same principles were used in each product to maintain consistency. Due to different needs, products vary in font size.

Typography

I chose my favorite icon library - Iconly 2.5

The icons are nested in their own 24x24px squares for consistent size and alignment.

For other and more "niche" icons, I used a plugin - Iconify with a huge resource of different icons

Typography

I designed the cards as components in Figma so that I could reuse them without wasting time creating them

In the design, I picked an 8-point. The main margins on the Landing Page were set to 150px. For the margins inside the groups I used 8 and 16, and the margins between the groups at 24, 32 and 40. In the project I used Auto Layout, which strongly speeds up the work, but checking the distances of the elements by a square with widths of 8x and 4x.

The Dashboard design has smaller spacing between elements, hence I used a 4pt grid accordingly.

Typography
